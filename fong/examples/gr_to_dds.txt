Fong & Spivak §3.11 — the running example.

  C = Gr (a directed graph: edges and vertices, with src/tgt arrows)
  D = DDS (a discrete dynamical system: a state set with a `next` arrow)
  F : Gr → DDS  collapses both objects to S, sends `s ↦ id` and `t ↦ next`,
  so a DDS-instance pulled back along F becomes its trajectory graph.

%% id: C
E -- s --> V
E -- t --> V

%% id: D
S -- next --> S

%% id: F
V -- F --> S
E -- F --> S
s -- F --> id
t -- F --> next
